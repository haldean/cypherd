cypherd - Simple text document distribution

Welcome to cypherd. cypherd is an online service that allows you to anonymously
share text files with the world. It can be used as an anonymous blog, or to
anonymously transfer encrypted files.  Documents are limited to 32 MB.

Get started by downloading our client script from http://root.cypherd.org/client
The client requires a POSIX-compliant shell, curl and (optionally) gpg.
